A small Kiosk near Spencers, Baishaki, this place serves South Indian dishes. Reasonably priced and the food is quite good. Have tried Idli, Vada, Dal Vada and Dosa. Love the red hot chutney they serve along with the sambar and coconut chutney. Good value for money.
