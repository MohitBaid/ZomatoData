3.5
Located right behind Quest Mall, this is a tiny shop. Air conditioned and clean, it has a variety of cakes, pastries and other savory items. The blueberry cheescake is very very soft and very very delicious. It, quite literally, melts in your mouth. Another favorite is the choco bite, which is a chocolate pastry loaded with chocolate goodness. The chocolate mousse deserves a special mention. It is served on a chocolate bowl, topped with chocolate ganache and chocolate chips. There were also a few chocolate pound cakes on display.
Coming to the savoury items, the cheese chicken puff is my favorite, along with the chicken cutlet. The chicken is soft and very flavorful.
The cons of this place are that it is really very small. There is just enough space to stand. Also, the prices are a little on the higher side but considering the taste, totally worth it. A slice of cheesecake and chocolate bite were for Rupees 45, the Chocolate mousse was for 50.
Definitely good enough for all those sudden chocolate cake cravings.
