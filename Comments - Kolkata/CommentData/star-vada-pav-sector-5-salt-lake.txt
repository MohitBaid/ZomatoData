Star Vada Pav is another budding vada pav serving chain just like Goli. And with the time passing by, it has successfully opened nearly six kiosk-type outlets across Kolkata. Happened to discover this outlet which surprisingly functions round-the-clock (24 Hours), silently pulling the fast-paced IT people and successfully impressing their tummies. Thus, drop by for a quick bite whenever in the area. About the fare, the menu had a number of different types of Vada Pavs to suit the varying palates of which the following were called for:

- Grilled Cheese Vada Pav
A crisp and non-greasy Vada made of mixed vegetables sandwiched along with a slice of cheese between a horizontally slit-bun with coriander chutney as a spread. Overall, tasted good.

- Tandoori Paneer Vada Pav
This was a bummer. The specimen picture and the received product were poles apart. The specimen pictures resembled the McSpicy Paneer of McDonald's but, the product wasn't anywhere near.
The received product consisted of a batter-fried patty/piece of paneer (cottage cheese), sliced cucumber and tomato sandwiched between a butter-grilled bun with mayo & coriander chutney as spreads. Did taste decent but, sorry I had ordered it looking at the specimen which had lettuce and no cucumber. False portrayal of products is one of the biggest sins a food outlet can commit. Disappointed.
Kindly check attached pictures for a clearer picture.

- Crunchy Veg Vada Pav
yet another huge disappointment. The specimen picture and the received product just like the above, were poles apart. The specimen picture had two slices of cheese, a patty, slices of cucumber, tomato, onion and iceberg lettuce. But, the product wasn't anywhere near.
The received product consisted of a crisp-fried patty, slices of onion and tomato sandwiched between a bun and served. WHERE ON EARTH DID THE REMAINING FILLING VANISH?!¬† Did taste average but, sorry I had ordered it looking at the specimen which had way more layers!! Yet again, False portrayal of products is one of the biggest sins a food outlet can commit. Disappointed.
Kindly check attached pictures for a clearer picture.

- Masala Vada Pav
Crunchy patty sandwiched in a bun along with chili garlic powder and ketchup. Tasted nice.
