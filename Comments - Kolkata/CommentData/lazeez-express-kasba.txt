The chicken cheese kebab served here is one the best in the city and yes, it beats the one served at Arsalan. 6 pieces of soft reshmi kebabs topped with well tandoor-ed and melted cheese. The texture is mouth melting and, the cheese is delicious. People not being able to tolerate cheese must stay away from this dish.

6) Chicken Bharta @Rs.170.00, (3.0/5), Oredered - 1 full portion: Tasted average. The gravy was sweetish with a little hint of spicyness. Shredded chicken pieces were ample in quantity. Topped with half a portion of boiled egg.

Not too spicy, not too bland! The biriyanis were nicely made, though the piece of Mutton was just bones and fat! The Kashmiri chicken was suppose to be less spicy, but we felt it was way too bland with no distinct flavour at all! Then for dessert we ordered for Firni! The Firni was again pretty impressive taste-wise! Could have been texturally better!
 Service is not slow, though not quick either! Value for money would be 4 out of 5! They also have a delivery counter called Lazeez express! I would like to try it out soon! 
 Over all a satisfactory experience! Do visit! Enjoy! :)
