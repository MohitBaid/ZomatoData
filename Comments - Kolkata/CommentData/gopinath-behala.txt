Gopinath is the best sweet shop that we have in the Behala area other than Rabindra sweets. All their sweets are fresh and the yogurt they prepare is absolutely lovely. Most of the sweet shops now put a lot of dalda in the curd to make it thick, but Gopinath's curd is very light and tasty. Also I love the Dahi Vada they make and their Chocolate sandesh as well as the GaWja. The samosas have a perfect blend of spices and badam in it and makes for a great evening snack.
