Haji Ali name seems to be a local low grade Biryani shop but this one is not. This one is an AC Restaurant opposite to Technopolis at Saltlake.

Location : Haji Ali is very easy to find, located beside famous Azad Hind Dhaba opposite to Technopolis at Salt Lake Sec 5.

Decoration : Straight roadside view, Simply decorated, Bright lighted, Neat & clean Dining that you can see from the road. Just beside the dining there is outdoor counter & you can see the grilled kebabs hanging in front of the kitchen too.

Staffs & Management : Young staffs are there. They are very helpful & quite fast to serve.

Foods : They have a lot to offer from Indian Tandoor to Chinese. But as per other reviews we also stuck to the Indian once. So we ordered the following...

Chicken Biryani : Chicken Biryani came in a Handi like the traditional way. It was not too rich & spicy but the spices were mixed together well. The rice quality was good & cooked well. Somehow the Biryani fragrance was missing & the rice felt little oily. Unfortunately the Chicken piece inside was worst of its quality. It was just steamed cooked without spices & absolutely tasteless from inside. Overall the Biryani doesn't came up to the mark.

Tandoori Chicken : Tandoori Chicken was served with sliced salad & green sauce like it should be. Tandoori was rich looking, very nicely grilled to red. It was soft from every angle & it also tasted brilliant. Just like every other review says about this restaurant, their Tandoori is really great & one of the best in its standards.

Chicken Tikka Butter Masala : 6 pieces of Chicken dipped into rich orange spicy gravy, garnished with cream & green leafs on top looked fabulous. Chicken pieces were very soft, very nicely cooked where the taste of the gravy came from inside. Just the right amount of oil & spices were used that made it felt very nice & tasty.

Tandoori Roti : Tandoori Roti came sliced in two parts. It was soft, nicely baked & tasted very good with the side dishes. The size of the Roti was big too.

Pricing : Pricing seems little higher than same kind of restaurants. However they doesn't add any kind of VAT with their bill, so its a huge plus point. You will only pay what it is printed on the menu card. Fortunately quality & quantity both justifies the price point well. All those above mentioned items for 4 people costs us Rs. 705.

Conclusion : Haji Ali at that position is a great option to choose from. Their Tandoori Chicken is a must try. With higher rating than its nearest competitor Azad Hind this one is great for trying Indian dishes.

This place is one of the hidden gems of Sector-5, Saltlake. It's a take-away/home delivery joint as of today, with few seating arrangements (stools,etc). But recently they are coming up with one dining area just adjacent to the present outlet, once this dining section is open this place will surely pull even more crowd.

a) Location - - - - Opposite Technopolis building, sector-5, Saltlake Kolkata.

b) Billing - - - - Let's talk about billing first since that is the first thing that takes place once you are here at this joint with your order. It's simple, fast and you are handed over your printed bill in a flash. And yes, as of today only cash is accepted which you need to pay right when the bill gets generated.

c) Service - - - - Once the bill is generated, your waiting time starts. I have tried this joint maybe 6-7 times, but this part is still a mystery to me. Some days they deliver stuffs in a minute or two, and on some occasions they take even 15-20 minutes to hand over the food (maybe with order of a Biriyani which is lying ready in front of them!). So I would say the service is really inconsistent at this place and needs to be improved a lot to build the goodwill. Though I must say the person (at the billing counter) dealing with customers is quite polite and prompt.

d) Food quality/taste - - - - And now comes the best part. The food at this joint is truly amazing and eclipses all other negatives. Try their double egg double chicken roll, it's really delicious and truly soul-satisfying! The paratha is not too deeply fried and is bit on the softer side, the way I like it. I have tried both Chicken Biriyani and Mutton Biriyani at this place, both are simply mouth watering. Infact the Mutton biriyani at Rs.130 is just amazing! It's absolutely stunning with awesome taste and attractive aromas. The mutton piece in there is insanely tender with an equally tasty and well-cooked potato. Only one point, the Mutton Biriyani at 130/- or Chicken Biriyani at 120/- doesn't contain any boiled egg in them.

e) Food quantity - - - - No issues with this part as well. The quantity of the biriyani is huge and should be good enough for one adult. The rolls are also quite filling, more so if it has egg in it.    

f) Hygiene - - - - The take-away joint which is there at present has no ambiance and you can directly view the kitchen. Hygiene can definitely be improved though it is quite okay considering it is (at present) just a street-side joint. Once their dining area is functional we can hope for better ambaince and hygiene at that place for sure.

Overall, this is one nice value for money joint, much better when compared to the other joints present in the vicinity. It's one of my favorite spots very close to my office for  evening snacks or lunch take-aways. Do try out their Biriyanis and Rolls, you surely won't regret.
