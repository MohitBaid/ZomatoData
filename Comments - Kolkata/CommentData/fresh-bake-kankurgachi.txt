Decor : From outside the store, its hoarding are flashy enough to attract people. Also one can see a level above the shop where foods are cooked. Inside the shop, there is a proper division for veg / non veg snacks and for eggless / with egg pasties cakes.

Staff : Staffs are friendly and happy to help you.

Food ordered : 
Caramel pastry
Pineapple pastry
Black forest
Chocolate brownies
Eggless chocolate muffins
Vegetable continental pie

Description : Fresh bake is a small joint at kankurgachi crossing. I regularly cross it on my was to office. Finally got a chance to try it. Firstly, they advertise it as live bakery. To me live bakery means cakes or patties are made live in front of us. But that's not the case, it is either already prepared or they make it fresh on order at the kitchen which is just above store. So not totally convinced with live bakery advertisement but ya they serve fresh.
Caramel pastry has caramel on top of the pastry, with coffee flavour in the sponge. Loved it. Must try.
Pineapple pastry n black forest were average.
Chocolate brownie & chocolate muffins were very good.
Vegetable continental pie is top notch. Must try. 
Overall a nice bakery. Don't be misguided by the word live bakery. Liked the division of veg/ non veg foods, I being a pure vegetarian. Recommended.
